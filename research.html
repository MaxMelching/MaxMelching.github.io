Science

On my scientific interests

I am mainly interested in Computational Physics and also statistics. A natural interest is therefore Gravitational (Wave) Physics, especially the Data Analysis involved.

As a part of that, I wrote my Bachelor's thesis on "Systematic Differences in the Source Properties of the third Gravitational Wave Catalog" in Frank Ohme's group at the Albert-Einstein institute (AEI) in Hannover. In 2024, I returned to Frank's group at the AEI for my Master's thesis on "Systematic Errors in Gravitational Waveform Models". Many of the repositories on my GitHub came into existence during this time, along with the thesis itself that was submitted in May, 2025. One of the repos is a PyCBC plugin that was developed as part of the paper arXiv:2503.17400.
