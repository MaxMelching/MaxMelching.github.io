Science

On my scientific interests

I am mainly interested in Computational Physics and also statistics. A natural interest is therefore Gravitational (Wave) Physics, especially the Data Analysis involved.
As a part of that, I wrote my Bachelor's thesis on "Systematic Differences in the Source Properties of the third Gravitational Wave Catalog" at the Albert-Einstein institute (AEI) in Hannover. For the thesis and code accompanying this work, click here. I have now (in 2024) started my Master's thesis on "Systematic Errors in Gravitational Waveform Models", returning to Frank Ohme's group at the AEI.
